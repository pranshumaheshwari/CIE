CIE

Governing Council of IIT Indore Incubation Center



Prof. Pradeep Mathur-Chairperson

Founder Director of IITI, Prof. Pradeep Mathur is an Indian organometallic and cluster chemist. He is a former professor of the Indian Institute of Technology, Mumbai and is locally known for his studies on mixed metal cluster compounds. He is an elected fellow of the Indian Academy of Sciences The Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, the apex agency of the Government of India for scientific research, awarded him the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology, one of the highest Indian science awards, in 2000, for his contributions to chemical sciences.

Dr. Avinash Sonawane

Convener of CIE, Dr. Avinash is an Professor at the Discipline of Biosciences and Biomedical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Indore. His research interests include Role of Mycobacterium tuberculosis glycoproteins and glycolipoproteins in modulation of host immune responses and Development of L-asparaginase based therapy for the treatment of primary and relapse acute lymphatic leukemia.

Dr. Bhargav Vaidya

He is an Assistant Professor at the Disciple of Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Indore. He is interested to study Astrophysical plasma flows using numerical simulations. In particular, his research interests include study of astrophysical jets from young stars and Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN), accretion disks, interaction of shocks in Astrophysical environments. His main focus has been to bridge the gaps between numerical simulations of Astrophysical plasma flows with their multi-wavelength observational signatures.

Dr. Hem Chandra Jha

Head of Discipline of BSBE, IIT Indore, Dr. Jha has research interests in Host-pathogen interaction- Epstein-Barr virus and Helicobacter pylori associated co-infection in cancer progression and Pathogens burden during viral infection of Gastric Cancer, Head and Neck Cancer and Multiple Sclerosis.
